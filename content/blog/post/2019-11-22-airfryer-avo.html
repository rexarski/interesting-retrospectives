Was inspired by an Hearthstone cardback artwork featuring avocado and egg, so I decided to make a new dish with my newly purchased air fryer.

Steps:

  1. Cut the avocado into halves.
  2. Remove the seed.
  3. Crack an egg and pour it onto the avo.
  4. Spray some salt, pepper and spices.
  5. Pre-heat the air fryer at 200 Celsius degree for 5 minutes.
  6. Fry the avo at 180 Celsius degree for 8 minutes.

Enjoy!
